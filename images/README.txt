Put product images here.

How to use:
1. Drop image files in this folder, e.g.  neck-fan.jpg
2. In products.js, set the matching product's  image: "images/neck-fan.jpg"

Tips:
- Square or 4:3 photos look best on the cards.
- Compress each image to under ~150 KB (use https://squoosh.app) so the page stays fast.
- .webp or .jpg are ideal.
- Social share preview: save one image as  og-cover.jpg  (1200 x 630 px) - it's already linked in index.html.

Until you add an image, each product shows a clean colored tile with its emoji - the page works fine with no images at all.
